# Awareness–Cognition Kernel & Jackson Response v2 — Reading Guide

## What These Documents Are

**`paper/awareness_cognition_kernel.tex`** — A 16-page paper applying the GCD kernel to 34 organisms across a 10-channel, 5+5 partition (awareness vs. aptitude). Proves 10 theorems (55/55 subtests), includes Monte Carlo robustness analysis (10,000 trials), partition sensitivity testing, and causal grounding. This is the methodological exemplar for how to build a Tier-2 closure that produces publishable, falsifiable results.

**`docs/jackson_response_ricp_v2_extended.md`** — A formal response to K.C.S. Jackson's "RICP v2 GCD Validation — Extended Findings" email. Assesses 10 claims against the kernel using the TYPE-A/B/C/D/P taxonomy, confirms 6, refines 2, rejects 3, and identifies 1 as not falsifiable. Provides constructive methodology guidance referencing the awareness-cognition paper as template.

## What They Are For

The **kernel paper** establishes the standard: blind channel assignment, prediction-first protocol, perturbation survival, partition sensitivity, falsifiable claims. It demonstrates that the GCD kernel discovers genuine structure in biological data (the inversion theorem, universal instability, three evolutionary strategies) when the methodology is rigorous.

The **Jackson response** applies that standard to evaluate an external contributor's work. It separates what is mathematically confirmed from what is post hoc, what is numerology, and what needs retraction — while preserving encouragement and identifying the most promising direction (multi-trace collective closure).

## Reading Order

1. **Awareness-cognition kernel paper** — Read first. It sets the methodological baseline. Pay attention to §Robustness Analysis (perturbation, partition sensitivity, causal grounding) — this is the template Jackson's work is measured against.

2. **Jackson response** — Read second. The verdicts in §I–§II only make full sense once you understand *why* blind assignment and perturbation analysis matter (demonstrated in the paper). §III ("The Methodological Gap") explicitly references the paper's methods as the path forward.

## How the Kernel Paper Helps

The kernel paper is not just a parallel study — it is the **standard of evidence** that the Jackson response enforces. Every correction in the response points back to a method the paper demonstrates:

- "Add perturbation analysis" → the paper runs 10,000 Monte Carlo trials
- "Use blind channel assignment" → the paper sources values from literature
- "State predictions first" → the paper predicts anti-correlation before computing it
- "Name what would break the theorem" → every theorem in the paper has explicit failure conditions

Without the kernel paper, the Jackson response would be a list of corrections. With it, the response becomes a constructive road map: *here is what rigorous looks like, here is where you are, here is how to close the gap.*
